Before You Contact Us

Quick answers to common questions

What Information Should I Prepare?

For IT Support:

  • Description of the problem and when it started
  • Any error messages you're seeing
  • Steps you've already tried to resolve the issue
  • How many users/devices are affected

For New Projects:

  • Your business goals and challenges
  • Current technology setup
  • Timeline and budget considerations
  • Number of employees and locations

For Consultations:

  • Brief overview of your business
  • Current IT pain points
  • Goals for improvement
  • Preferred meeting format (in-person, video, phone)
