About the Shop

Built on
Trust &
Hard Work

For over 15 years, E.A.C. Repair has been the go-to mobile equipment service in Western New Brunswick. Our reputation was earned the old-fashioned way — showing up, doing the job right, and charging fairly.

We bring a fully equipped service truck directly to your farm, home, or business. No loading, no transport costs, no waiting for a shop appointment. When your equipment goes down, we come to you.

From Woodstock to Hartland and every rural road in between, we've built something worth being proud of: a business that genuinely helps its neighbours keep working.
